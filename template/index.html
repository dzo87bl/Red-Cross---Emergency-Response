Survival Rule of Threes

In any extreme situation you cannot survive for more than:

  • 3 seconds without hope,
  • 3 minutes without air,
  • 3 hours without shelter,
  • 3 days without water,
  • 3 weeks without food.

Remember these simple tips when making your kit:

  • With bag space at a premium, clutter and free up space with consider packing multipurpose items.
  • Your kit should include 3 to 21 days supply.
  • Keep loose items in airtight plastic bags.
  • Gather the kit's items in easy-to-carry bag.
  • Store your kit in a convinient place known to all family members.
  • Put them within reach, near the exit you use most often.
  • Keep a smaller version of the kit in the trunk of your car.
  • Check, rotate, refresh and update your kit at least once a year.
  • This kit can not guarantee your survival

Building your kit with fallowing modules:

  • Bottled water and ready-to-eat, non-perishable food
  • Clothes and shelter
  • Hygiene and first aid
  • Communication and signaling
  • Documents and money
  • Tools
